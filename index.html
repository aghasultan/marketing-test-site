05Conversion-Rate FAQ

1 What is conversion rate & how do I track it?

Conversion rate = conversions ÷ sessions × 100. In GA4 create a destination or event goal (e.g. “/thank-you”) and view the % in Reports → Engagement → Conversions.

2 Why don’t visitors convert & how do I fix it?

Top issues: slow load, unclear value prop, weak trust, form friction. Fix: speed-optimise, rewrite headline around benefit, add testimonials/logos, cut form fields.

3 How do I A/B-test headlines, CTAs, forms?

Use Google Optimize or Optimizely → split traffic 50/50 → run until you hit statistical significance (power calculator). Iterate winner into new control.

4 Mobile CRO best-practices?

Single column, 44 px tap targets, sticky bottom CTA, compressed WebP images, test on BrowserStack.

5 How do pop-ups & exit-intent boost CR?

Offer a lead magnet after 5–8 s or 30 % scroll; exit-intent triggers when cursor leaves viewport. Use ConvertBox or Sleeknote; never block first-paint on mobile.
